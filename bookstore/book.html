Harry Potter and the sorcerers stone

Author: J.K. Rowling. | Price: $35

The *Harry Potter* series, written by J.K. Rowling, is a world-renowned fantasy book collection that follows the life and adventures of a young wizard named Harry Potter. Harry discovers that he is a wizard on his 11th birthday and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where he befriends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley. Throughout the series, Harry battles the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, who killed his parents and seeks to conquer the magical world. The books explore themes of friendship, bravery, love, and the battle between good and evil. The seven-book series has captivated millions of readers worldwide, leading to film adaptations, theme parks, and a lasting cultural impact.
